Handwashing Location Reminders App Privacy Policy

Handwashing Location Reminders App asks for permission to access the location of your device.
This information is used to decide when to remind you to wash your hands.

Location information is only stored locally on your device and is not accessed by the creators of the app in any way.
If you delete a reminder, the location associated with that reminder will be forgotten as well.

Handwashing Location Reminders contains third-party advertisments. Information collected by this app is
not given to third parties for advertising or any other purpose.

Contact handwashing-reminder-app@googlegroups.com with questions/concerns
