Made for the GMTK Game Jam 2020

Parasite

The theme for the GMTK Game Jam 2020 was "Out of Control"

Parasite is a fast-paced roguelike in which you play as a parasite rushing to escape your underground prison. Take control of hosts, jumping from victim to victim as they try to stop your escape. Once in control, shoot-em-up until you find the next host. But be careful... if you run out of control, it's game over.

Android App

Handwashing Reminders

An app that allows you to set up reminders to wash your hands. Choose to be reminded:

  • When you arrive somewhere.
    • Like when you get home from being out or when you arrive at work.
  • At a certain time
  • Periodically throughout the day.

Make sure you spend enough time washing your hands with the newly added timer!
